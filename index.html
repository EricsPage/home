Flying Wing

This is an RC flying wing I made from scratch. It used a KPM2 wingfoil. It can be contructed with two pieces of DollarTree $1 foamboard. The stencil from my cad design is included if you want to cut it out yourself. The reflective tape is to keep it from overheating in the sun haha.
